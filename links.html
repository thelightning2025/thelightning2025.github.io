Title: The Bookmark

Genre: Mystery/Thriller

Logline: A young librarian discovers a mysterious bookmark hidden in an old book, leading her on a quest that unveils a web of secrets, deceit, and a hidden treasure tied to her family's past.

Script:

INT. LIBRARY - DAY

The camera pans through the dusty aisles of a historic library. A YOUNG LIBRARIAN, EMMA (late 20s, curious and meticulous), is shelving books.

EMMA
(sighing, as she places a book on the shelf)
Another day, another shelf.

As she turns to leave, a book slips from the shelf, landing open. A peculiar, old-fashioned bookmark sticks out from between the pages. Emma picks it up, intrigued.

EMMA
(examining the bookmark)
What’s this?

The bookmark is engraved with an intricate design and a faded inscription: “Find what is lost, and you’ll find what is hidden.”

INT. EMMA’S APARTMENT - NIGHT

Emma studies the bookmark under a magnifying glass. Her curiosity is piqued.

EMMA
(to herself)
What’s the story here?

She starts researching the inscription and the book it came from.

INT. LIBRARY ARCHIVE ROOM - DAY

Emma discovers that the book is connected to a series of mysterious disappearances and rumors of a hidden treasure. She uncovers a photograph of a young woman who looks eerily similar to her.

EMMA
(whispering to herself)
Could this be connected to my family?

INT. ABANDONED MANSION - NIGHT

Emma, following clues, enters a decrepit mansion linked to the treasure. She navigates through dusty corridors, uncovering hidden rooms and cryptic messages.

EMMA
(determined)
I’m getting closer.

She finds a secret compartment containing old letters and a map. The letters reveal a family secret: her great-grandmother had hidden a valuable artifact.

INT. LIBRARY - DAY

Emma returns to the library, now a hero. She places the bookmark in a glass case, as a tribute to her great-grandmother and the legacy of the hidden treasure.

EMMA
(smiling to a colleague)
Sometimes, the smallest things lead to the greatest discoveries.

The camera zooms in on the bookmark, now prominently displayed.

FADE OUT.

The Bookmark combines elements of mystery and personal discovery, leading to a thrilling resolution that ties past and present together.
